How It Works

Enter Your Home Address

  • Your address can be anywhere… so long as anywhere is located in the Garden State!
  • Upon entering your home address, you’ll be immediately directed to our Offer Questionnaire
  • Once your address is in, you’re on your way to your no obligation cash offer!

Fill out the Questionnaire

  • We want to find out as much as humanly possible about your home, so we can give you an offer you’ll be happy with!
  • By accurately telling us about your home, we’ll be able to provide you with the most competitive cash offer
  • You don’t have to fill out every detail… but the more you tell us, the better your offer will be!

Receive your Non-Binding Cash Offer

  • Once we’ve analyzed the condition of your home in your market, we’ll send back your offer ASAP!
  • Upon acceptance of your no obligation offer, we’ll stop by for a quick on-site visit, to verify a few facts about your home!
  • With our process, you’ll be closing within a matter of weeks, and ready to tackle the next phase of your life with cash in hand!
